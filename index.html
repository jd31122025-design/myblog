My desk has several drawers, and I encourage you to snoop in the ones you find interesting.

Drawer 1: Blog »

This section of the website is dedicated to my life as a writer and my experience with self-publication. Writing is not an easy craft and, I believe, a writer never stops learning. I share my writing and publication journey - this includes the highs and the lows, so be warned.

Drawer 2: My Books »

Find out more about me and my books in this section of the website. I mainly write fantasy and adventure books, but I also have a dark fiction book too. All the books can be purchased via the links to online bookstores. All are under AU$5 and they are an instant download, unless you purchase a paperback version of course.

Drawer 3: For Writers »

This section of the website has recently changed the most. I have removed all the old writing resources and will add up-to-date tips for new writers in the future.

Drawer 4: For Readers »

If you like to read, then you will be interested in the book reviews and author interviews found on this page. We all want free ebooks, so I will share links to websites where you can download them legally.

Drawer 5: Archives »

Sometimes it is fun to dig deep into the archives of a site and see what turns up. If you enjoy doing that, here is a link to all the topics and categories you can find on this website. Enjoy!

Drawer 6: Contact »

If you see something wrong on the website (such as an error or a broken link), please let me know. Or, if you would like to send me an email for any other reason, please feel free to do so. I will endeavour to respond promptly.

And two last things:

I want to bring your attention to two important pages — my [[Terms and Conditions]] and [[Privacy Policy]]. Rest assured, regardless of what normally happens when you visit a website, this is a static website that doesn’t involve a dynamic or interactive element. There is no need for you to login or confirm your identity for any reason. I do not track your data for advertising or analytics purposes and have no way of checking that information anyway, even if there were cookies collecting the information. And if I had access to the information I would not and would never share it with anyone. Honestly, unless you send me a message via the contact form, I have no idea who has been here and/or when. Any information provided via the email form will be used for the purposes of reading your message and sending a response, if required, and then immediately deleted.

And last, but by no means the least important, I want to give a shout out to all the folks at Talk Tiddlywiki for their time, specific code, patience and continued advice, which helped me create this website. I love the result. Thank you.

Thank you for stopping by.

...Karen
